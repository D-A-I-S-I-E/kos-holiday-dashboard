🏖️ Beach Recommendations

  • Paradise Beach – sandy, lively, good for a classic beach day.
  • Agios Stefanos Beach – scenic, calm, with views of Kastri islet.
  • Tigaki Beach – long beach, good for swimming and sunset walks.
  • Therma Beach – natural hot springs, best for something different.

🍽️ Restaurant Shortlist

  • Barbouni – seafood by the water.
  • Avli – Greek food in Kardamena.
  • Elia – traditional Greek setting.
  • Broadway – modern Greek food in Kos Town.
